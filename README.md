# Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze sales performance, product profitability, and customer behavior from a retail Superstore dataset.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and advanced analytics. The objective is to help business stakeholders monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

## Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data across products, customers, and regions. Without proper analysis, identifying profit drivers, loss-making products, and customer trends becomes challenging.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing:

* Sales and profitability monitoring
* Product performance evaluation
* Customer segmentation analysis
* Regional sales comparison
* Business insight generation through data visualization

## Key Business Insights

### Sales Performance

* Technology category generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Certain regions consistently outperformed others in both sales and profitability.
* Sales showed seasonal fluctuations indicating demand trends.

### Product Insights

* Several products generated high sales but low profitability due to heavy discounting.
* Loss-making products were identified for further pricing and inventory review.
* High-performing products contributed significantly to overall profit.

### Customer Insights

* Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
* A small group of customers accounted for a substantial portion of revenue.
* Customer purchasing behavior varied significantly across regions.

### Profitability Insights

* Increased discounts did not always lead to increased profits.
* Some categories maintained strong margins despite lower sales volumes.
* Profitability varied across states, highlighting opportunities for regional optimization.
